Data Sources

Data Ordering

  • CloudSat. CloudSat products can be ordered from the CloudSat DPC upon registration. Registration and ordering is free.
  • CALIPSO. CALIPSO products can be ordered via NASA Earthdata. Registration and ordering is free.
  • MODIS. MODIS products are available through the LAADS Web, even without registration.

Data Browsing

Data previews can be browsed on the web at the following locations.

  • CloudSat. CloudSat products can be browsed on the web through the Quicklook Images section on CloudSat DPC.
  • CALIPSO. Products can be browsed on the CALIPSO project website at NASA LARC.
  • MODIS. You can use the MODIS Rapid Response System to browse MODIS imagery.
